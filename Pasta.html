Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti|| 2 eggs|| 100g Pecorino Romano cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Whisk the eggs and grated cheese together.
  3. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the egg mixture.


Enjoy your Spicy Pasta!
