Chicken Biriyani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt || 500g chicken pieces || 2 cups Basmati rice || 2 sliced onions || 2 chopped tomatoes || 2 green chilies || 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves || 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves ||

    2 sliced onions || 2 chopped tomatoes || 2 green chilies || 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves || 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves || 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee

Instructions:

  1. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Marinate chicken and refrigerate.
  2. Boil rice with cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and salt until 70-80% cooked. Drain.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown; set half aside for garnish.
  4. In the same pan, add whole spices, then chicken. Add tomatoes, green chilies, coriander, and mint. Cook.
  5. Layer half the rice, chicken, herbs, and fried onions. Repeat layering. Garnish with fried onions.
  6. Seal the pot and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes. Fluff rice and serve hot.


Enjoy your Chicken Biryani!
