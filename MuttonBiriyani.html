Mutton Biriyani

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams (about 1 pound) mutton, cut into pieces|| 1 cup plain yogurt|| 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste|| 1 teaspoon red chili powder|| 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder|| Salt to taste

    2 cups long-grain Basmati rice|| 2 large onions, thinly sliced|| 2 tomatoes, chopped|| 2 green chilies, slit|| 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped||

    1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped|| 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee (clarified butter)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Add the mutton pieces to the marinade and mix well.
  2. Cook the rice until it's 70-80% cooked (it should still have a slight bite). Drain the rice and set it aside.
  3. Add the marinated mutton and cook until it changes color and is mostly cooked through.
  4. In a large heavy-bottomed pot, layer half of the partially cooked rice. Spread the mutton mixture evenly over the rice layer.
  5. Serve hot with raita (yogurt side dish) or a salad.

Enjoy your Spicy Mutton Biryani!
